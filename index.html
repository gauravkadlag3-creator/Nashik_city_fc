About The Club

Nashik City Football Club is committed to developing football talent from grassroots to competitive level. Our aim is not only to create better players, but also disciplined, respectful and confident individuals.


At Nashik City FC, we focus on technical training, fitness, match awareness, teamwork, leadership and character building.
